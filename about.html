About Me

Engineering background in energy systems and electrical engineering

My background connects renewable energy engineering, electromechanical system design, and electrical engineering analysis. I am currently pursuing an M.Eng. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at McGill University. My current interests focus on power systems, circuit simulation, control systems, hardware design, and practical engineering system integration.
